FAQ

Common questions about our web scraping services.

Is the data delivered in a usable format?

Yes — all data is delivered as clean JSON or CSV, ready for direct import into spreadsheets, databases, or your application. No messy HTML or parsing required.

How long does a typical scrape take?

Small datasets (under 500 results) are usually delivered within 24 hours. Larger datasets (10,000+ records) typically take 24-48 hours depending on the target website.

Can you handle websites with anti-scraping protection?

We use Apify's infrastructure which includes proxy rotation and browser automation to handle most anti-scraping measures. For highly protected sites, additional pricing may apply.

What's included in the price?

One-time scrapes include the raw dataset, one format conversion (JSON or CSV), and one free revision round. API access and ongoing scheduled runs are available on custom plans.

Do you offer ongoing data monitoring?

Yes — for recurring needs, we can set up scheduled scrapes with periodic delivery (daily, weekly, or custom). Contact us for a custom quote on monitoring plans.

Are your scrapers legal to use?

We scrape only publicly available data. Customers are responsible for ensuring they comply with the terms of service of the websites they're scraping. We do not bypass paywalls or login barriers.

Can I see a sample before ordering?

Yes — most of our scrapers have free test runs available on Apify Store. You can also request a free sample of up to 10 records before placing a full order.

Do you work with non-English websites?

Absolutely. We've scraped websites in Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Spanish, German, French, and more. Multilingual data extraction is one of our specialties.
